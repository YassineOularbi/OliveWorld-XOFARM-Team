ABOUT XO-FARM

FARM AND COMPANY

Welcome to XO-FARM! We're a family-owned farm dedicated to producing the highest quality olive oil. Nestled in a region known for its flourishing olive groves, we uphold generations-old traditions in olive cultivation and oil production. We prioritize sustainable farming practices to preserve our environment and heritage. At XO-FARM, we hand pick olives and meticulously oversee every step of production to ensure each bottle of our extra virgin olive oil captures authentic taste and rich aromas. Our commitment to excellence shines through in every drop, offering chefs, home cooks, and food enthusiasts alike a taste of superior quality and tradition. Join us in supporting responsible agriculture and savoring nature's best at XO-FARM.
